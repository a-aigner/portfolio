How it works

Creating a bet takes seconds. Here's the magic:

1

Share content

Tap the share button in any app and select Bets from your Share Sheet.

2

Add your bet

Describe what you're betting on and set the stakes. Get creative!

3

Send to group

Pick your group and watch the bets roll in. May the best friend win.
